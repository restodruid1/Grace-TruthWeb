Who We Are

About Our Church

Rooted in Scripture. Committed to community. Compelled by grace.

Our Mission

What We're About

"To know Christ and make Him known — reaching our community and the world with the grace and truth of the Gospel."

Everything we do flows from this mission. We gather to worship, we scatter to serve, and we grow together along the way. Our prayer is that every person who walks through our doors — or joins us online — would encounter the living God and find their place in His family.

What We Believe

Our Core Values

These values shape how we worship, how we serve, and how we treat one another.

📖

Scripture

We believe the Bible is the inspired, authoritative Word of God and the foundation for all we believe and do.

✝️

Grace

We are saved by grace alone, through faith alone, in Christ alone. Grace is not just our doctrine — it's how we live with one another.

💡

Truth

We are committed to teaching the whole counsel of Scripture honestly, even when it challenges us and calls us to change.

🤝

Community

We were made for relationship. We pursue genuine friendship, mutual accountability, and deep belonging with one another.

🙏

Prayer

We believe prayer is not preparation for the work — prayer is the work. We are a people who depend on God in every season.

🌍

Service

The church exists for those who aren't yet part of it. We serve our neighbors, our city, and the nations with the love of Christ.

Our History

Our Story

Grace and Truth Chapel began with a small group of families who shared a simple conviction: that the local church, rooted in Scripture and filled with the Spirit, is still God's chosen instrument to change the world. In the early days, we met in living rooms and community halls — just a handful of believers committed to one another and to the mission.

Over the years, God has grown this congregation in ways we never anticipated. Families have been restored. Prodigals have come home. People from every background have found community here. Through seasons of growth and seasons of difficulty, our faith has been tested and strengthened.

Today, Grace and Truth Chapel is a multigenerational, multiethnic community of disciples. We haven't arrived — we're very much still on the journey. But we are deeply grateful for every step the Lord has led us through, and we are full of anticipation for what He has ahead.

If you're reading this, we believe you're here for a reason. We'd love for you to be part of the next chapter of our story.
