A Place to Belong

You Are Welcome Here

At Grace and Truth Chapel, we believe the church is not a building; it’s a people. We’re a community of imperfect people following a perfect God, and there is always room for one more.

Whether you’re exploring faith for the first time, returning after time away, or looking for a church home, we’d love to meet you. Come as you are.
